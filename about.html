About Grand Alpha Exim

Three generations of agricultural knowledge, transformed into a modern export company.

Who We Are

Grand Alpha Exim is a trusted and fast-growing export company from India, specializing in fresh vegetables, fruits, rice and agro-based commodities. With three generations of farming and agricultural expertise, we understand both the field and the global market.

Founded in 2021, we started as a packaging and aggregation firm supporting major exporters. Because of our focus on quality, transparency and commitment, we naturally expanded into full-scale export operations under our own brand.

Our Mission

To deliver high-quality, chemical-free, farm-fresh produce to global markets with integrity, consistency and competitive pricing.

Our Vision

To become one of India’s most trusted food export companies by maintaining consistent quality, ethical sourcing and reliable shipment timelines.

Our Values

Honesty in business, respect for farmers, long-term partnerships with buyers, and continuous improvement in processes.

Farm to Freight Model

We work directly with farmers and collection centres, removing unnecessary middlemen. This ensures:

  • Better prices for farmers
  • Fresher products for buyers
  • Full traceability from farm to shipment

Packaging & Quality

We follow international packaging standards:

  • 5kg / 10kg / 20kg cartons
  • Mesh bags, LDPE bags, HDPE sacks
  • Vacuum packing on request
  • Clear labelling with product, grade, origin and packing date

Every consignment passes through grading, sorting and quality checks before loading.
